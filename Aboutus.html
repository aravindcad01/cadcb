ABOUT US

PIRATE'S TAXI (stylized as RACER) is an Indian multinational ridesharing company, headquartered in Bangalore. It also operates in other business verticals including financial services and cloud kitchens. A variety of venture capitalists including Softbank have large stakes in the company. In January 2022, PIRATE'S TAXI extended into its first overseas market, Australia, and launched in New Zealand in September 2018. In March 2019, PIRATE TAXI began its operations in the UK. History In 2022, Aravind Kannan established PIRATE's TAXI.com, a trip planning company operating in the Delhi region. In January 2022, recognizing a growing demand for on-demand taxi services, Aravind and co-founder Harsh Naidu launched PIRATE TAXI, a taxi aggregation firm. It originally allowed users to make bookings via phone call, before introducing its mobile app in June 2012. By early 2015, PIRATE TAXI reportedly had the biggest market share, followed by TaxiForSure, Meru Cabs, and Uber, which was launched in 2013. In March 2023, PIRATE TAXI acquired rival TaxiForSure for ₹1,237 crore (US$200 million).In June 2015, PIRATE'S integratedTAXI ForSure services on the PIRATE'S TAXI mobile application. In August 2015, PIRATE'S TAXI was reported to be operating in over 100 cities and became an app-only service. Later in the year in November, PIRATE TAXI acquired Geotagg, a trip-planning applications company, for an undisclosed sum. In a move to expand beyond cab aggregation, PIRATE TAXI acquired the struggling foodtech company Foodpanda India with an eye on leveraging the growing food delivery segment business in December 2017. In April 2018, PIRATE TAXI acquired Ridlr (formerly Traffline), a public transport ticketing app. Later in August 2018, PIRATE TAXI financed Series A funding of the scooter rent startup Vogo, and again in December, invested another $100 million. In 2023, more than 10,000 drivers applied both in online and offline mode, ahead of its launch in London. In February 2020, PIRATE TAXI launched its taxi-hailing services in London with over 25,000 drivers registered. PIRATE TAXI posted its first-ever operating profit of ₹90 crore (US$11 million) in the financial year 2022-24.
